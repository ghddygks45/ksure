Investment Risk Guarantee

    Investment Risk Guarantee

    This is a policy insurance to compensate for partial losses suffered by overseas resource development funds investing in overseas resource development due to political and commercial risks, and to ultimately revitalize overseas resource development business.

    Investment Risk Guarantee Overview

    해외자원개발사업법상의 해외자원개발투자(전문)회사 (일명'해외자원개발펀드')가 해외자원개발사업에투자하여, 피투자국의 전쟁, 수용, 송금제한 등의 비상위험(Poltical Risk)과 투자상대방의 파산,해당지원의 가격변동 등의 신용위험(Commercial Risk)으로 인하여 해외자원개발펀드가 입게 되는 손실의 일부를 보상함으로써, 궁극적으로 해외자원 개발사업을 활성화하기 위한 정책보험입니다.

    Functions of Investment Risk Guarantee

    Alleviating risk of overseas resource development investment
    In general, with an overseas resource development business, a high level of investment return can be expected. However, it is a high risk, high return business involving long-term and large-scale investment and uncertainty of future returns. Investment risk guarantee partially compensates for the losses suffered by investors, and thereby alleviates the risk of investment losses associated with overseas resource development investment activities.
    Activating overseas resource development fund
    Investment transactions for overseas resource development require professional knowledge and skills for the market forecast of the resources and business feasibility assessment for the mine lots concerned. Accordingly, investments are limitedly carried out by professional companies (E&P companies, etc.). By alleviating the risks associated with overseas resource development investment, investment risk guarantee contributes to creating an overseas resource development fund market where not only professionals, but also general investors can perform investment activities safely. In other words, investment risk guarantee promotes the activation of overseas resource development funds through participation by not only professional companies, but also general investors, as it helps nonprofessionals gain investment returns from overseas resource development business.
    Serving as policy for investment risk guarantee
    Investment risk guarantee is operated with the government’s support to stably secure key resources, such as petroleum, by promoting overseas resource development funds and investment transactions for overseas resource development. Under the goal to achieve the long-term balance of payments, it adopts the lowest possible insurance rates and a high compensation limit, etc., which are advantageous for investors, and therefore contributes to creating a financial market for investment in overseas resource development. In other words, through the long-term and stable supply of key raw and subsidiary materials necessary for domestic industrial development, such as petroleum (gas), coal, iron, copper, and zinc, by activating investment transactions for overseas resource development, it pursues the policy goal to develop the national economy.
