Import Insurance (For Importer)

    Overview of Scheme

    This scheme is to indemnify losses caused by a domestic importing company becoming unable to recover prepayment due to political or commercial risk in an import transaction under the condition of prepayment for the long-term stable supply of key strategic items, such as petroleum and gas.

    Product Structure

    Large view of images

    Target Transactions

    Import transactions for which the following items must be shipped within two years from prepayment (excluding intermediary trade)
