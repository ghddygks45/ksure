    Overseas Debt Collection Support
    With its accumulated expertise, K-SURE supports the collection of overseas account receivables generated through export transactions.

    Why K-SURE?

    ※ Service charges are incurred at the debt collection agency service stage, and not imposed at the consultation stage.

    Procedure of service use

    01Application for consultation
    • Are you worried about overseas accounts receivables? Apply for consultation!
    Apply for online consultation
    02Consulting for debt collection strategies
    • An assigned consultant will help you with customized collection strategies according to the characteristics of the accounts receivable.
    If necessary, use “Overseas Debt Collection Agency Service”
    * What is the overseas debt collection agency service?

    K-SURE serves as an agency to collect overseas accounts receivables through its overseas network

    03Overseas Debt Collection Agency Service* Application and Registration
    • Following the first consultation, send the required documents to request the debt collection agency service.

      * Overseas Debt Collection Agency Service: K-SURE serves as an agency for collecting overseas accounts receivables through its overseas network

    04Debt Collection
    • Debt collection is executed by stage, and the progress is informed.
    05Success/Failure and Termination of Collection
    • If collection is succeeded, the recovered amount and service fees are settled, and if collection is impossible of not effective, the collection is terminated.
