Comprehensive Service Insurance (Interim/Deferred Payment Basis)

    Overview of Scheme

    This scheme is to cover losses caused by domestic exporters becoming unable to recover their export proceeds after exporting services, such as system integration, technical services, contents, engineering, etc.
    크게보기
    • K-SURE
      Service export credit insurance (insurance proceeds paid in case of non-paymentby importer)
    • Domestic exporter
      Service export
    • Overseas importer
      Pay export proceeds

    Target Transactions

    • System integration services (hardware, software, system networks, IT personnel for the establishment and maintenance of information system)
    • Medical service, finance, transportation, tourism, performance, design, education and consulting services
    • Export of contents(on-line and mobile games, movies, animations, etc.) and software
    • Technical services, such as engineering, industrial property rights
    • Services of Industry convergence, etc.
