    1992 (since the establishment of K-SURE foundation) - present

    This is the history of the Korea Export Insurance Corporation (KEIC) and the Korea Trade Insurance Corporation (K-SURE)

    The Korea Trade Insurance Corporation was founded in 1992 as a government-funded organization for exclusively handling and operating the export/import insurance scheme.
